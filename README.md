## Summary
Tic-tac-toe is a paper-and-pencil game for two players, **X** and **O**, who take turns marking the spaces in a 3 Ã— 3 grid. The game may be started either by the **X** player or by the **O** player. The player who succeeds in
placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row wins the game.
